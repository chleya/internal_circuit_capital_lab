## 5. Representation / Readout Caveat

> This capture records `state_last` (last token hidden state) and
> `state_window4` (mean of last 4 tokens during prefill, or single token
> during decode). The choice of readout position could affect downstream
> analyses. If a behavior signal is concentrated in non-last positions,
> `state_last` may underrepresent it. The `state_window4` partially
> addresses this during prefill but is identical to `state_last` during
> decode steps (only 1 position available). Future work should consider
> cross-position readout strategies for decode steps.
